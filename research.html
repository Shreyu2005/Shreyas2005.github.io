Research

I am an undergraduate researcher interested in how semiconductor materials, interfaces, and device structures translate into measurable electrical and optical behavior. My work focuses on learning and applying TCAD-based simulation workflows using Synopsys Sentaurus, combined with analysis and visualization using Sentaurus Visual, Python, and LabVIEW.

Multi-Quantum Well (MQW) TCAD Setup
Sentaurus SDE + Sentaurus Device

As part of my undergraduate research, I developed TCAD simulation setups for multi-quantum well (MQW) structures to explore how layered material systems influence band structure and carrier behavior.

  • Constructed layered well and barrier geometries using Sentaurus SDE.
  • Enabled HeteroInterface and QuantumWell models in Sentaurus Device to study band-edge profiles and carrier confinement.
  • Analyzed outputs including conduction/valence band energies, carrier density, electric field, and interface-driven trends.

Photodetector Simulation: Optics + EQE Workflow
Sentaurus Device Optics + Sentaurus Visual

I worked on simulating a semiconductor photodetector to understand how optical excitation translates into device response using TCAD optics and post-processing tools.

  • Configured OpticalGeneration models and wavelength sweeps in Sentaurus Device.
  • Applied RayTraceBC boundary conditions to study reflectance and absorption effects.
  • Used Sentaurus Visual to post-process simulation data and compute trends in quantum efficiency (QE) and responsivity.

Bandgap / Model Parameter Studies (I–V Analysis)
Sentaurus Device + Sentaurus Visual

To strengthen my understanding of device physics, I conducted parameter studies using different bandgap and intrinsic carrier concentration model settings in a diode simulation environment.

  • Configured bandgap and recombination-related parameters in Sentaurus Device.
  • Solved coupled Poisson and carrier transport equations for steady-state behavior.
  • Generated and analyzed I–V characteristics using Sentaurus Visual.

Visualization, Automation & LabVIEW
Sentaurus Visual + Python + LabVIEW

Alongside TCAD simulation, I worked with visualization and automation tools to make results easier to interpret and present clearly.

  • Used Sentaurus Visual to extract cutlines, band diagrams, and device-level plots from simulation outputs.
  • Developed Python scripts (Pandas/Matplotlib) to process exported data and generate clean plots.
  • Utilized LabVIEW for data visualization and analysis workflows supporting interpretation of electrical and simulation-based datasets.
